Embracing Autism

Understanding and supporting young learners with autism

Explore topic outline

Explore the course outline prepared to give you a strong foundation in mastery

Course Description

  • This course is aimed at informing and raising awareness on the characteristics of young learners within the autism spectrum disorder and how they may present unique instructional differentiation in educational settings.

  • Practical and teacher-friendly educational strategies in helping learners within the spectrum achieve optimal success in school will also be covered.
What will students learn in the course?
  • Identify behavioural characteristics of autism
  • Describe goals and environments that best support young learners with autism
  • Discuss educational strategies that help young learners with autism effectively
Are there any course requirements or prerequisites?
  • None
Who can join the course?
  • Open to anyone who is interested to learn more about teaching young learners with autism.
