Diploma in E-Commerce

What is E-Commerce?

Have you ever wondered how to start your own online business or how international brands have embraced E-Commerce as part of their retail strategy? E-commerce is a sales channel that enables entrepreneurs and businesses to sell online, directly to consumers, but that’s not all. It is also about marketing, gaining consumer trust and confidence and so much more; without physically interacting with customers both locally and beyond geographical borders.

E-commerce has great future prospects as most businesses are moving online for greater penetration into the market through faster and wider reach via the usage of the Internet. The programme will provide students with an in-depth understanding of the context, nature, role and significance of e-business / e-commerce activities in a dynamic business world. Students will gain exposure to theoretical and practical knowledge of conducting an online business over the Web and also the fundamentals of entrepreneurship for the opportunity to start their own businesses.
