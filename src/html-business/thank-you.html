Why Study Business with MCKL?

Industrial Revolution 4.0 (IR 4.0) is upon us and has impacted societies, business models, future jobs and careers. As more emphasis is placed on smart solutions, automation, data analytics and internet of things (IoT) technology in businesses, the demand for digitally-skilled talents are also widely growing.

To meet the demands of IR 4.0, MCKL recognises the need to equip our students with future-oriented skills. Our new diploma programmes are here to prepare our students to be job-ready!

Gain Future-ready Skills & Knowledge

Exposure to industry-relevant skills such as Search Engine Optimisation (SEO), Social Media Content Marketing, International Business, Cybersecurity and Blockchain.

Progress into an International Degree

Opportunity to pursue a bachelor's degree at our partner universities abroad, such as Sheffield Hallam University, Deakin University and Keele University.

Be Job-Ready with Industrial Training

12-week industrial training in the final semester with industry experts provides real-life working experience and a seamless transition to employment.
