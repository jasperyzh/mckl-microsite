What is Financial Technology (FinTech)?

Imagine 10 years ago, would you have ever guessed that we are en route to becoming a cashless society or that financial instruments no longer require banks or face to face interactions to take place? This is FinTech. FinTech, or financial technology, is used to describe businesses that use technological innovation to enhance or automate their financial services and products. This allows for quicker processes, personalised services, and more accurate data. Technology in finance continues to evolve due to advances in big data, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning and data science. FinTech is now being used in various areas of finance such as mobile banking, cryptocurrency, blockchain, investment and trading.
