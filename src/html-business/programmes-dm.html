What is Digital Marketing?

Have you ever wondered what goes on behind the scenes of every Chinese New Year video or Hari Raya contest that you see online? Digital marketing is the combination of various marketing effort that takes place online in one form or another. By leveraging various digital channels such as search engines, email, social media, websites, and mobile apps, brands are able to reach out to their target audience much more easily, efficiently as well as precisely than ever before. From creating brand awareness to driving sales, digital marketing is now where traditional advertising used to be.
