Professional Accounting Programmes with MCKL

ACCA Foundation in Accountancy (ACCA FIA)

KPT/JPS(R2/344/3/0183)(A9696)01/24

Intake: January, April & July 2022

ACCA FIA is the entry level qualification consisting of 7 modules. It is the fast-tracked route that allows students to complete the ACCA Qualification in just 3 years.

Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA)

KPT/JPS(R/344/6/0064)(FA1206)06/22

Intake: January & July 2022

ACCA programme is designed to equip students with in-depth knowledge, techniques, and skills in all accounting areas, including financial accounting, assurance, taxation and financial management.
